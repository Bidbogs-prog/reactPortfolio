---
title: "Little Corner"
date: "2026-04-02"
kind: "verse"
summary: "A short one about building quiet things on the internet."
tags: ["Poetry"]
---

I keep a little corner of the web,
swept clean each morning, lit at night,
where everything I half-believe
gets argued into something right.

No crowd arrives. That isn't why.
I build the way a person prays —
to leave one honest, working thing
behind the noise of all my days.

So if you're here, you wandered in.
Stay a while. The kettle's on.
I'll show you what these hands have made
before the corner, and I, move on.
